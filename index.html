Arkansas, The Natural State

5 important facts of Arkansas

  • The population of Arkansas is 3,067,732, according to the United States Federal Reserve
  • President Andrew Jackson approved a bill creating the State of Arkansas on June 15, 1836.
  • Arkansas is located in the south central region of the United States.
  • Arkansas incorporats a blend of urban, suburban, and/or rural communities throughout its state.
  • As of June 2024, ZipRecruiter reports that the average annual salary in Arkansas is $63,314

The Natural State

Arkansas is known as "The Natural State" because of its natural beauty, including its mountains, forests, rivers, lakes, and wildlife. The state's diverse geography ranges from the Ozark and Ouachita Mountains to the Mississippi River Delta.
